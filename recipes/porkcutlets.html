Pork Cutlets

Pork Cutlets is a simple and delicious dish

Ingredients you will need

  • Pork meat
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Bread scrubs

Steps

  1. Smash the meat with your full strength
  2. Season the meat
  3. Cover it in that order flour,egg,bread scrubs
  4. Fry it on the pan for 3 minutes each side
  5. Voila! your food is done
