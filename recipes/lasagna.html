Lasagna

Lasagne is an italian dish with a very nice flavour and is relatively easy to make

Ingredients you will need

  • Pasta plates
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Passata
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Minced meat

Steps

  1. Cook your plates
  2. Slice the onion
  3. Stir onion with the meat
  4. Add your passata on the pan and season it
  5. Make a layer on your plates with meat, bechamel sauce and Cheese
  6. After all the layers are made, cook it in the oven for 45 minutes in 180 degrees
  7. Voila, your lasagne is done!
