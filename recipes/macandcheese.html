Mac and Cheese

Mac and Cheese is a simple dish from USA, known for its simplicity and flavour richness

Ingredients you will need

  • Pasta
  • Cream
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the Pasta
  2. Add pasta in the pan
  3. Add the cheese and cream
  4. Mix it for the next 5 minutes
  5. Its done! bon appetite
